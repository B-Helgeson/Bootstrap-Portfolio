About Me

Hello, My name is Brandon Helgeson (HEL-guh-sen). I am a Senior Business Analyst at a major theme park company. My job entails wearing a lot of hats; I am a Product Owner for an Agile Scrum team, I help to manage technical teams, I configure park tickets and research questions in a database, I help to ensure that the cutting edge new technologies being delivered are inline with the requirements of Marketing, Finance, and Operations, among many, many, other things.


Outside of work, I am a part-time student at the University of Central Florida, where I am currenty enrolled in a six month "coding bootcamp" program, aimed at becoming a full-stack web developer. When I am not working or coding, I enjoy SCUBA diving, fishing, and other activities that take me outdoors in beautiful central Florida. I also love to learn new grooves on the drumset, restore old things, and build new things.
